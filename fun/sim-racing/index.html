Sim Racing

Fun > Sim Racing

Real racing is not on the cards, so I do it virtually. I help run NOMAD Sim Racing. Spiffing website, don't you think?

I've been sim-racing since sometime in 2015, progressing from a little xbox steering wheel to a fully built out sim rig with VR. Getting into the sim with my racing buddies is a way to feel like I'm doing something fairly productive when really I'm just playing a game. A nice mid-point between the "mind on" of work and the "mind off" of running.
