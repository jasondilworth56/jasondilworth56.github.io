Need an audience?

I have spent the vast majority of my professional life working in a marketing capacity, most notably as the founder of a successful e-commerce only agency as well as as the Technical Director at The Marketing Eye.

My specialisms form around data collection, both of a new audience as well as their habits as you engage them through a funnel.

What I don't focus on:

  • amount of new traffic (although new traffic does come through channels like Google Ads)

What I do focus on

  • quality of traffic
  • conversion rate of traffic

If you feed more quality into the top of the funnel, more quality appears at the bottom – as long as the right steps are taken throughout.
